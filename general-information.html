Prices of services

The prices of the services offered in Israel are usually lower than the official prices of the service provider abroad (for example: compared to the price list of the hotel or car rental company). However, it is possible that at some point in time the prices in Israel will be higher.

The prices of the services may change without prior notice and in any case, the determining and binding prices are the prices in the country.

Price guarantee

The price increase will apply to all bookings that have already been made, unless they have been paid in advance in full and the travel documents (flight ticket, hotel voucher, car voucher, etc.) are already in the passenger's hands. If there is a discrepancy between the prices listed on the website and the prices at the reservation center, the price at the reservation center is the determining factor
